# What's new in Myna 0.2

A faster, prettier, more useful Myna.

## Highlights

- **Speaks within ~240 ms.** Press the hotkey and the first word lands
  almost four times faster than before. Long passages stay smooth —
  Myna prefetches the next chunk while the current one plays.

- **New menu bar.** A custom popover replaces the plain menu. Now-playing
  card, voice picker, speed chips, the last five things Myna read.
  Every button labeled.

- **Floating playback pill.** A small pill appears at the bottom of the
  active display while Myna reads. Hover to expand into a mini player.
  Drag it anywhere — Myna remembers where you put it. Pin it always-visible
  from Settings → Advanced.

- **First-run welcome.** Fresh installs get a 60-second spoken
  introduction that walks you through Myna and asks for the permissions
  it needs.

- **Per-app voices (Wardrobe).** Pin a different voice per app —
  Bella for articles, Joe for code, Anna for chat. Settings → Wardrobe.

- **Trackpad gestures (beta).** Four-finger tap to speak the selection,
  four-finger double-tap to stop. Off by default — turn it on in
  Settings → Gestures.

- **Claude Code toast cards.** When a Claude Code hook fires, a small
  playable card slides in from the top right. One tap to play; auto-tucks
  away after 8 seconds.

- **Karaoke ribbon (opt-in).** A companion sidecar shows the current
  spoken line at the bottom of your screen. Toggle from the menu.

- **Voice previews.** Audition voices before committing. Settings → Voice.
  Ducks any in-flight audio while previewing.

- **Quiet language hint.** If you ask Myna to read non-English text it
  surfaces a "Detected: ES — adjust voice in Settings" chip. Never
  auto-switches voices on you.

## Quality

- Visible loading state the moment you press the hotkey — no more
  wondering whether Myna heard you.
- Menu-bar icon now reflects state at a glance: idle, thinking, speaking,
  paused, error.
- The floating pill correctly appears on whichever display your cursor
  is on, not always the built-in screen.
- Fixed an animation bug that could spike CPU on the menu-bar icon.
- A few rough edges in voice selection, recents tracking, and the
  status display.
