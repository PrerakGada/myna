## Highlights

- **A real menu.** The popover now has a state-driven top section
  (Now Reading / Paused / No audio playing), live hotkey labels on
  the transport rows, and a Recent submenu of the last five things
  Myna read.
- **A thoughtful bird.** The menu bar icon shifts through five
  states — idle, speaking, thinking, paused, error — with a soft
  halo when Myna is waking up the engine.
- **Claude Code toasts.** When Claude finishes a long task, a small
  toast slides in from the top-right of your display with the
  project's color and a one-tap Play. Stacks up to three; the
  rest sit in the menu bar.
- **Voice previews.** Try a voice from Settings → Voice before
  committing to it.
- **What's New, on every minor update.** You're reading it.

## Behind the scenes
